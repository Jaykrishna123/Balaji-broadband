WHO WE ARE AND WHAT WE DO

We have been providing digital cable TV service from last 24 years. Balaji Cable initially entered into cable industry in 1994 in a small corner of Antophill i.e Vijay Nagar. We was the first to introduce the cable tv in our zone . From its period onwards to uptil now it was well known changes happened in our systems due to which we have climbed where we are uptil now.

Balaji Internet started enhancing his business in the year 2008 Balaji Internet is a well known for broadband services in wadala region. Balaji Broadband, a brand owned by Live Line Network Technology Pvt Ltd is a Category VNO- CLASS ‘B’ Internet Service Provider that delivers a high-speed seamless internet experience for both home and corporate users.

Balaji offers flexible blend of service to the clients ranging from Individuals, Professionals, Small and Medium Businesses houses, Big Corporate houses and Public Sector. We pride ourselves in maintaining transparency across services delivered to customers and providing unmatched customer service
