What is Balaji Fiber is offering?

What is a gigabit?

One gigabit per second (Gbps) is a 1000 Mbps (Mbps) connection. With Fiber 1000, you’ll get Internet speeds up to 1 Gbps. That means enough Internet for everyone in your home—and their devices. Everyone at home can have a super fast connection on their computers, smartphones, tablets, and more—at the same time.

Why do faster Internet speeds matter?

Faster Internet speeds will help revolutionize the web in new ways. Imagine chatting with your doctors or teachers via HD video conference, or collaborating on a work or school project with contributors across the globe in real time. Imagine new online billing applications that don’t freeze. Or automatically saving all of your work to the cloud in a blink of an eye—and not having to worry about losing files in a computer crash. Faster speeds mean more possibilities.

What are the advantages of Broadband?

Broadband allows you to complete your task of downloading or uploading large-sized files or multimedia content web pages much faster than
Dial-up Internet,2G and 3G connection and thereby saving your valuable time, and giving you a superior internet experience.

Will I get the speed according to my plan through Wi-Fi?

Not necessarily.The internet speed is fastest when the device is directly connected to an Ethernet cable .
The speed may drop when connected through Wi-Fi due to wireless interfence or due to incorrect router.

What is leased line?

Leased Lines are business grade circuits that provide guaranted, dedicated and symmetrical bandwidth.
Perfectly suited for organization that depend on 100% connectivity,leased line don't suffer from shared bandwidth.
Leased line is the only solution that guarantees that the internet connection speed you pay for,is the speed you get.

What is a Wireless broadband connectivity?

Fixed wireless broadband provides high speed Internet access without cable,fiber or phone-line connections.
Data is securely transmitted wirelessly for two-way communications cable for data video and voice.
